Who Am I?

Hello! I’m Adhil Biju, a passionate tech enthusiasist and continuous learner from the vibrant state of Kerala, India.
I completed my higher secondary education at St. George HSS Kattappana in Idukki, Kerala, where I laid the foundation for my academic journey.
Currently, I am pursuing a bachelor's degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering at the National Institute of Technology, Calicut.
Beyond academics, I am an avid gamer and sports enthusiast, always eager to engage in activities that challenge both my mind and body.
My passion for technology drives me to constantly explore the latest trends and advancements in the field.
I pride myself on my strong problem-solving skills and enjoy creative pursuits such as editing.
As an ambivert, I thrive in both collaborative and independent environments, and I am always open to exploring new opportunities in the ever-evolving tech industry.
My aspirations include mastering full stack development and DevOps, as I am determined to deepen my expertise and make a meaningful impact as a passionate techie!
